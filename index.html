Graduate Recital

an evening of clarinet music
Andrew Seohyeon Kim
— Clarinet —
with Edward Holly — Piano —

For four wonderful years here in Ireland, I have been blessed to study, grow, and share the music I love with so many remarkable people.

This graduate recital closes that chapter, and it would mean the world to share this final evening with you — with all my thanks for being part of the journey.

You are warmly invited.

— a project of mine —
Classical Music
for Everyone
A community music project I began in 2024, built on a simple belief: classical music should not live only in concert halls, but be encountered as a shared experience in everyday life — accessible to anyone, regardless of age, background, or familiarity with the music. It works to lower the barriers, to stay alongside the people and communities it meets, and to trust that small moments of listening together can shape a kinder community.
